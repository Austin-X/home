Evolution of Video Games

Video games was invented in the early 1950s, when scientists invented simple video games and simulations. It became popular during the 1970s to 1980s because of arcades, controllers, and computers. Since the 1980s, video games became a very trendy form of entertainment. Popular video games emerged, such as Pac-Man. From 1983-1955, the third generation of video games were introduced, which were 8-bit units. The fourth generations of consoles were invented in 1987-1999 and the fifth generation, which were 32 and 64 bit units, emerged from 1993 to 2006. The eighth generation of consoles were introduced in 2013, including Nintendo Wii U, Xbox one and Playstation Vita. As you can see, video games are develepoing at a very high rate as the years progress.

Counter Strike Global Offensive is about a multiplayer first-person shooter game that requires strategy and skill to win. The objective in this game on the counter terrioists side is to kill the terrioists, wait until hte time runs out, or defuse the bomb. At the end of each round, the currency is rewarded depending on the player's performance in the last round.
